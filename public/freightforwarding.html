Tema Port directly contributes to the economic growth of Ghana.

Over 50 Shipping lines provide both direct and indirect shipping services to and from the Port of Tema. Tema Port operates an Integrated Management System in line with ISO 14001:2015 (environmental management), ISO 9001:2015 (quality management) and OHSAS 18001:2007 (occupational health and safety). Tema Port is the gateway to most of landlocked neighbours i.e. Burkina Faso Mali and Niger. Transit corridors to neighboring countries no longer have inspection barriers which hitherto caused unnecessary delays. The cost of cost of transportation to the landlocked countries via the Port of Tema is a sound business choice.
