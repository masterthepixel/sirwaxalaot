What is a ceramic coating?

A Ceramic Coating, such as Ceramic Pro is a liquid polymer that is applied by hand to the exterior of a vehicle. The coating chemically bonds with the vehicle’s factory paint, creating a layer of protection.

No other coating comes close.

A Ceramic Coating is not a substitute for paint protection film, which provides a more comprehensive for of defensive. Rather, it is a premium wax alternative. A Ceramic Coating creates a permanent or semi-permanent bond with a vehicle’s paint, meaning that it does not wash away or break down and does not require repeated application every few months.

  • Is Ceramic Pro safe for my paint or clearcoat?

    Yes, Ceramic Pro Products are formulated to be safe for all surfaces including your paint. Ceramic Pro will not damage your clearcoat or paint and will not void any manufacturer warranties.

  • Does Ceramic Pro fade, crack, peel, or yellow?

    No, Ceramic Pro will not Crack, Peel or Yellow. Our products have undergone SGS testing and real-life testing in some of the worlds harshest environments, and surpassed every one of them.

  • Will Ceramic Pro protect my vehicle from scratches?

    Yes, after Ceramic Pro coating cures is becomes 3 times harder than paint. The coating is much more resistant to scratching. Ceramic Pro is a glass coating, and while glass can still be scratched and chipped, it is much more resistant than your clearcoat.

  • Will Ceramic Pro protect my vehicle from rock chips?

    Ceramic Pro is a Silica based product, meaning it is a “glass” coating. Ceramic Pro adds some rock chip resistance, but it can still get rock chips from larger stones and high speeds. The glass windshield on your vehicle will still get rock chips, just much less often than your clearcoat.

Your investment deserves the absolute best.

This makes it the hardest paint coating on the market today. This new glass shield prevents minor scratches to your clearcoat and acts as a sacrificial layer. This preserves the original factory paint.
